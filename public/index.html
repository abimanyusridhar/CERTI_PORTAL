About This Certificate

The Cyber Security Threat Awareness Training (CST) certificate is issued by the to vessels that have successfully completed the mandatory maritime cybersecurity training programme, organized under the supervision of ISO Lead Auditors and certified Security Trainers.

Compliance is aligned with international maritime regulations and is valid for one quarter following completion. Port State Control (PSC) officers, vetting inspectors, and compliance managers can independently verify the authenticity and current validity of any CST certificate issued by .

Validated Competencies
Regulatory Frameworks
Proficiency in navigating ISM Code, ISPS, and NIS2 compliance requirements as they apply to maritime operations.
Threat Intelligence
Ability to identify maritime-specific risks, including AIS/GPS manipulation, OT-targeted ransomware, and phishing vectors.
Operational Security
Mastery of maritime cyber hygiene, including secure media handling, password management, and social engineering defense.
Emergency Preparedness
Knowledge of incident response and recovery protocols to ensure vessel safety and operational continuity post-incident.
