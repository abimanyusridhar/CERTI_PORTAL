About VAPT Assessment Certificate

The Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing (VAPT) certificate is issued by the following a comprehensive on-vessel cyber risk assessment. It confirms that the vessel has undergone a structured evaluation across IT/OT systems, access controls, software configuration, and cyber resilience practices in compliance with IMO mandated cybersecurity requirements.

Assessments are fully aligned with IMO Maritime Cyber Risk Management guidelines (MSC-FAL.1/Circ.3), the ISM Code (SOLAS Chapter IX), ISO 27001:2022, and OWASP standards. The certificate is valid for one year from the date of assessment and is recognized by SIRE / CDI vetting inspectors, Port State Control (PSC) officers, Flag State authorities, and charterers as documented evidence of proactive vessel cyber risk management. Verify any certificate instantly by entering its VAPT certificate number above.

VAPT assessment records contain sensitive cybersecurity findings and are classified as RESTRICTED. Access is limited to authorised maritime compliance and vetting personnel only.

Assessment Scope
Access Control Review
USB / removable media, data transfer, login credentials, domain accounts, email access, and vessel asset controls reviewed, tested, and hardened against breach scenarios.
IT/OT Risk Analysis
Full cyber risk analysis of operational technology (ECDIS, AIS, GMDSS, engine control) and shipboard IT systems per IMO and BIMCO guidelines.
Software Version Control
Verification of patch status, firmware currency, and version management for all IT and OT platforms aboard, including critical navigation and safety systems.
Backups & Disaster Recovery
Disaster recovery planning, backup schedules, restoration drills, and business continuity protocols assessed against industry best practice.
Crew Awareness & Drills
Shipboard crew cybersecurity awareness, IT emergency drills, phishing simulation results, and internal audit compliance verified.
