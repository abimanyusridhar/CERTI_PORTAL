About VAPT Assessment Certificate

The Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing (VAPT) certificate is issued by the Synergy Cybersecurity Team following a comprehensive on-vessel cyber risk assessment. It confirms that the vessel has undergone a structured evaluation across IT/OT systems, access controls, software configuration, and cyber resilience practices.

Assessments are aligned with the IMO Maritime Cyber Risk Management guidelines (MSC-FAL.1/Circ.3), ISO 27001:2013, OWASP, and applicable flag state requirements. The certificate is valid for one year from the date of assessment, and is recognized by vetting inspectors, PSC officers, and charterers as evidence of active cyber risk management.

Assessment Scope
Access Control
USB / Data / Login / Domain / Email / Asset controls reviewed and hardened.
IT/OT Risk Analysis
Full cyber risk analysis of operational technology and shipboard IT systems.
Software Version Control
Verification of patch status and version currency for IT and OT platforms.
Backups & Recovery
Disaster recovery planning, backup schedules, and restoration drills assessed.
Awareness & Drills
Crew cybersecurity awareness, IT drills, and internal audit compliance.
